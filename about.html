About

Blueberry Rouge are a band from Dublin, Ireland. With roots in soul, funk, folk and jazz they've brought together a sound of their own. Donegal's Ronan Friel meets Pittsburgh's Jennifer Waters on vocals and Carlow's Ryan O'Neill on drums.
